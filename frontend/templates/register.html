1. Service Description

ImproveCommunication is an AI-powered English language learning platform that provides real-time conversation practice, analysis, and personalized feedback.

2. Language Requirements

English-Only Communication: All conversations, practice sessions, and communications on this platform must be conducted exclusively in English. This is essential for:

  • Accurate AI analysis and feedback
  • Consistent learning experience
  • Fair leaderboard competition
  • Proper grammar and fluency assessment

3. Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect:

  • Audio Recordings: All your voice conversations and practice sessions are recorded and stored
  • Text Transcripts: Audio is transcribed for analysis purposes
  • Performance Metrics: Grammar scores, fluency ratings, vocabulary usage, pronunciation data
  • User Activity: Session duration, practice frequency, quiz results

4. Data Usage

How We Use Your Data:

  • AI Analysis: Your conversations are analyzed by AI to provide grammar, fluency, and pronunciation feedback
  • Personalized Recommendations: Your data is used to generate customized learning paths and targeted quizzes
  • Performance Tracking: Historical data helps track your improvement over time
  • Platform Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used to enhance our AI models

5. User Responsibilities

By using this platform, you agree to:

  • Speak only in English during all sessions
  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Respect other users in peer-to-peer practice sessions
  • Not use offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language
  • Not share your account credentials with others

6. Leaderboard and Gamification

Your performance scores, practice time, and achievements may be displayed on public leaderboards. You can opt out of leaderboard participation in your profile settings.

7. AI-Powered Features

Our platform uses artificial intelligence for:

  • Speech-to-text transcription
  • Grammar and syntax checking
  • Fluency and pronunciation analysis
  • Personalized quiz generation
  • Improvement suggestions

AI analysis may not be 100% accurate and should be used as a learning aid, not absolute truth.

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your active account plus 90 days. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support.

9. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms, including but not limited to: using languages other than English, sharing inappropriate content, or attempting to manipulate the system.

10. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
