üí° Why Do Both Tabs Show the Same User?

When you login in one tab, the authentication token is saved in localStorage. All tabs from the same browser share the same localStorage. That's why when you refresh or open another tab, it shows the same user.

Solution: Use incognito mode or different browsers, which have separate localStorage!
